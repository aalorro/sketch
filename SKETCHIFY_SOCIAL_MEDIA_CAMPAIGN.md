## FINAL TIPS

1. **Authenticity First**
   - Share real struggles (took "X hours to build this feature")
   - Show real user reactions
   - Be transparent about future roadmap

2. **Consistency Over Perfection**
   - Post regularly, not perfectly
   - Quick phone videos beat perfect 4K footage (if you're behind)
   - Authenticity > Production value

3. **Community Over Metrics**
   - Build genuine connections, not follower counts
   - Respond to every comment for first 24 hours
   - Ask for feedback constantly

4. **Timing is Everything**
   - Post when your audience is active (generally 6-9 AM, 12 PM, 6-9 PM)
   - Test different times and double down on winners
   - Use platform peak times: TikTok (6-10 PM), Instagram (11 AM-3 PM, 7-11 PM)

5. **Adapt Quickly**
   - What works in Week 1 might flop in Week 3
   - Look at analytics after each post
   - Double down on formats/topics that win
   - Be willing to pivot
